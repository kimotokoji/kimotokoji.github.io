Research Interest:#

Development/improvement of advanced transmission electron microscopy#

  • Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM) and Electron Energy-Loss Spectroscopy (EELS).
  • Scanning transmission electron microscopy (STEM).
  • 4D-STEM with unsupervised machine learning.

Microscopic characterization of advanced materials#

Ceramics, oxides, nitrides, strongly-correlated-electron materials, superconductors, dielectric materials for semiconductor devices, metallic glass, 2D materials, etc.

Keywords:#
